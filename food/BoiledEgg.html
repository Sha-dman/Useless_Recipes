Egg

About:

This recipe will teach you how to boil an egg from scratch!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Bowl
  • Fire
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Get your Live chicken and raise it to where it can lay its own eggs
  2. After you find the chicken mature enough to make its own eggs, leave the chicken alone and let it do its thing
  3. After months of waiting you finally got you egg.
  4. Now you need water, if you need help making that, follow this link, not too hard. heat the water and wait till it boils
  5. Place the egg in the water and wait about 5-7 minutes, time may vary depending on if you want salmonella, so play around with it
  6. Finally, peel the egg like ... an egg. Your adults you should know this, if not try this video.
