Pizza

About:

This recipe will show you how to GET pizza

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (except PINEAPPLE)

Directions

  1. Get your water, if you need help wiht that try this recipe.
  2. Mix that with your flour and knead it until you get dough.
  3. Now place your dough on your counter, but cover your counter with flour before hand.
  4. Flatten out your dough and make it like a pizza.
  5. Spread your sauce and sprinkle your cheeze, be genererous.
  6. Add your toppings, I prefer eggs, if you want you can follow this recipe.
  7. Prehead your oven to the temperature of the earths core and add your pizza.
  8. Afterwards, your pizza should look like asphalt.
  9. Finally you can call Dominos and order yourself some proper pizza, ENJOY!
